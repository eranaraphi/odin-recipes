Red Sauce Pasta

is a popular pasta variant made with a bright, tangy homemade tomato sauce. This tomato based sauce is usually made by saut√©ing onions & garlic in olive oil to which fresh or canned tomatoes or tomato puree is added. The ingredients are further cooked until the sauce thickens.

Ingredients

  • 2 kg pasta
  • 1 kg ground beef
  • 1 kg ground pork
  • 2 big packs of Hunts Tomato Sauce Parmessan
  • 4 bottles of Hunts Tomato Ketchup
  • 1 small pack of tomato paste
  • 3 blocks of cheese
  • 7 cloves of garlic minced
  • 7 big red Onions minced
  • 1 block of butter

Steps

  1. add ground beef and port to the pan
  2. add salt and pepper to the ground meet
  3. add 1/4 cup of water and boil the meat until dry
  4. add a bit of cooking oil and fry the ground meet until golden brown
  5. remove the ground meet and set aside
  6. Using the same pan, saute chopped onions with 1/4 butter until translucent
  7. add in the garlic until color is golden brown
  8. add the meet to the sauted garlic and onion
  9. add in 2 cubes of beef cubes and mix until completely dissolved
  10. add in 2 packs of Hunts tomato sauce
  11. add in 4 bottles of Hunts tomato ketchup
  12. add in tomato paste
  13. grate 2 blocks of cheese and add to the mix
